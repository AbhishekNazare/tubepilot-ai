# Title And Thumbnail Strategy

CTR measures whether a viewer chooses the video when YouTube gives it an impression. A low CTR usually means the topic, title, or thumbnail is unclear relative to alternatives.

## Thumbnail Rules

- Use one dominant idea.
- Make the emotional or practical contrast visible.
- Avoid tiny text that cannot be read on mobile.
- Do not repeat the exact title in the thumbnail.

## Title Rules

Good titles create a specific reason to click. They can promise a result, reveal a mistake, compare options, or create curiosity. The title should not overpromise beyond what the first minute proves.

If CTR falls far below the channel average, test a clearer title-thumbnail pair before changing the whole content strategy.
