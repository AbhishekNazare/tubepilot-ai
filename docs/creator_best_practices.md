# Creator Growth Best Practices

A strong creator system has three loops: topic selection, packaging, and viewer satisfaction. Analytics should be interpreted through those loops rather than as isolated numbers.

## Topic Selection

Choose topics that connect to a viewer problem, recurring curiosity, or current demand. A topic that performed well once should become a repeatable format only when retention and subscriber conversion also look healthy.

## Packaging

The title and thumbnail should create one clear question. Avoid crowding thumbnails with too many words or visual objects. The title should identify the viewer benefit or tension quickly.

## Viewer Satisfaction

Retention, comments, likes, and subscriber movement show whether the video delivered on the promise. A video with low subscriber gain and high subscriber loss should be reviewed even when views are acceptable.
